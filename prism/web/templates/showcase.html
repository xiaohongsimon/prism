How it works · pipeline

sources.yaml
    │    136 × {x, youtube, xiaoyuzhou, reddit, arxiv, github, …}
    ▼
[sync]       fetch raw items
    │
    ▼
[cluster]    dedup + semantic grouping
    │
    ▼
[analyze]    local LLM → summary · why · strength · tags
    │
    ▼
[pairwise]   Bradley-Terry + your 1-bit choices → preference model
    │
    ▼
[decision]   adjust source weights · pick new sources · flag anomalies
    │        (everything logged to decision_log for auditability)
    ▼
signals → /feed · /briefing · /showcase
